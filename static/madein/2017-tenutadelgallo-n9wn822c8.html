The Relais La Tenuta del Gallo is a charming agriturismo with 9 rooms set on a hill overlooking  20 hectares of farm land near Amelia. Each room oozes elegance and attention to detail. The accompanying Italian garden with its fragrant flower beds, a grove of oaks and chestnut trees, and lake. From the infinity pool you can enjoy a magical view of the valley. The Tenuta del Gallo restaurant offers a range of local cuisine to satisfy the most demanding palates. Among the many experiences that are offered guests are a the truffle hunt in a 70-hectare wildlife reserve with expert guidance and his faithful dog, including truffle-based lunch in a typical restaurant in the woods; rafting and riding; cooking classes and participation in the various activities of the farm, such as olive and fruit harvesting.
